Pizza recipe

Description

a flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin, consisting of a thin layer of bread dough topped with spiced tomato sauce and cheese, often garnished with anchovies, sausage slices, mushrooms, etc.

    Ingredients:

  • Flour.
  • Water and yeast.
  • Tomate sauce and cheese.

    Steps

  1. Mix flour, water and yeast and make the pizza dough.
  2. Put into the oven until the pizza dough is cooked.
  3. Add cheese and tomate sauce and let into the oven for 10 minutes.
